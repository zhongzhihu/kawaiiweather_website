FAQ

Beauty draws attention. Clarity keeps it useful.

Are images available for every place?

Not always. Major cities and well-known destinations are more likely to have ready-made imagery, while smaller towns and lesser-known places may not. When a specific location is not covered, the app can create an original AI-generated scene or fall back to artwork inspired by the iconic nature of the wider local region. Image generation is available as part of a premium subscription.

Where does the weather data come from?

Kawaii Weather supports trusted providers like Apple WeatherKit, Foreca, and official national meteorological agenciesnational meteorological agencies such as MeteoSwiss, Deutscher Wetterdienst, MET Norway, the Finnish Meteorological Institute, GeoSphere Austria, the Netherlands’ KNMI, Environment Canada, and the Japan Meteorological Agency.
