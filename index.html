FAQ

Beauty draws attention. Clarity keeps it useful.

Are images available for every place?

Not always. Major cities and well-known destinations are more likely to have ready-made imagery, while smaller towns and lesser-known places may not. When a specific location is not covered, the app can create an original AI-generated scene or fall back to artwork inspired by the iconic nature of the wider region. Image generation is available as part of a premium subscription.

Where does the weather data come from?

The app can work with the weather provider you trust most. That could be a platform service such as Apple WeatherKit or data from national meteorological agencies like MeteoSwiss, Deutsche Wetterdienst, Norway’s weather services, Finland’s agencies, Austria’s providers, or the Netherlands’ KNMI. The visual layer stays consistent while the forecast source remains flexible.
