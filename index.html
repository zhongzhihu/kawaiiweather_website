Can the app use location-based images like the reference site?

Yes, as long as the images are properly licensed or created for your product. The safest route is owned artwork, commissioned pieces, stock with app rights, or generated imagery with clear commercial terms.

Does the site need to look exactly like Acme?

No. This page keeps the same high-level rhythm of hero, showcase, features, and call-to-action, but the copy, layout, styling, and product story are original.

What makes this feel premium?

Strong typography, dramatic color, confident spacing, and a simple message: weather information should feel as beautiful as the places it represents.
