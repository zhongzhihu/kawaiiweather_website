Privacy for Kawaii Weather

Kawaii Weather ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information in a simple and limited way.

Last updated: March 24, 2026 No account required No cross-app tracking

1. Information We Collect

Kawaii Weather does not require an account or registration.

  • Saved locations and app preferences are only stored locally on your device.
  • When you request weather for your current location or for a searched place, the relevant location or place query may be sent to weather providers only to return the forecast.
  • If you contact us by email, we receive your email address and the contents of your message so we can reply.

We do not maintain user accounts or store your saved locations on our own servers.

2. Third-Party Services

Kawaii Weather uses third-party weather services to provide forecasts, including Apple WeatherKit and Open-Meteo.

These services may receive the location or place query needed to answer a weather request. Kawaii Weather does not use external analytics or cross-app tracking services.

3. Ads and Tracking

Kawaii Weather may display in-app ads, but it does not use your data to track you across third-party apps, websites, or services for targeted advertising.

Ads are intended to be contextual or otherwise non-personalized.

4. Children's Privacy

Kawaii Weather is intended for general audiences and is not specifically designed for children.

Because the app does not require accounts and is designed to minimize personal data handling, its data practices are limited. If you believe a child has sent us personal information by email, contact us and we will review the request.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. If changes occur, we will update this page with a new effective date.

Contact Us
